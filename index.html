Foundation

Trained in circuits, tuned for competition.

Ashrith Puppala is an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at PES University, Bengaluru — working across hardware sensor fusion, machine learning integration, and applied environmental research.

Before university, he topped the ISC Science Stream at St. Joseph's Boys' High School with a 98.2% board score. That same precision now shows up in multi-sensor systems that geotag potholes in real time and piezoelectric rigs that turn footsteps into usable current.

Off the breadboard: acrylic painter with work shown in public exhibitions, and a state-level badminton runner-up.
